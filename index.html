The sky was a kaleidoscope of colors as the sun shone brightly in the sky.The black and white,majestic mountain ranges were waves in a sea of freezing-cold snow. The peaks of the mountains were as spiky as a million knives poking out, like hands, reaching out for the sun. Green moss stuck on the rocks like glue. A lake of crystal-clear water flowed gently against the breeze down into an elegant waterfall to the remains below. A narrow path of sand slithered through the valley. The air was fresh and crisp, as snowy white birds tweeted and squawked, and swiftly glided over the mountains migrating to the other end of the world as summer was coming.
